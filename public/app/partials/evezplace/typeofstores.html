Types of Eve-Stores

We offer three type of stores - Basic Store, Marketplace Standard Store and Customised Store. Basic store is full featured store with visibility limited to closed private community of the store owner and it resides on member's Mysite. Paid standard provides full features and visibility in browse store section on Marketplace. Apart from providing customer features, Customised Stores provides unlimited visibility with visibility in browse store section on Marketplace with customised priority on Evezown landing pages as well.
